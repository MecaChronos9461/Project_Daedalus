Making CNC programming accessible through tactile Braille pieces and a magnetic board.

## üéØ Overview
Project Daedalus transforms CNC and programming education into a **tactile and inclusive experience**.  
It allows students who are blind or visually impaired to build CNC programs using **Braille blocks** arranged on a **magnetic board**, simulating the structure of real G-code and logic.
